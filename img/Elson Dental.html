Welcome to Elson Dental Office

We have provided family and cosmetic dentistry in Markham for over 25 years.

About the Dentist




Dr. Pranab Chakravorty

Dr. Pranab Chakravorty was born in London, England and completed all studies in Toronto, Canada. He graduated with Doctor of Dental Surgery at University of Toronto in 1990. Dr. Chakravorty is committed to providing everyone with exceptional care in a relaxed environment. In his spare time, Dr. Chakravorty enjoys spending time with family, golfing, swimming, travelling, singing and watching his favourite Blue Jays and Maple Leafs.
